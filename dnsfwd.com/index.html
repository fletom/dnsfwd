DNSFwd

A simple service that does all kinds of HTTP redirection goodness just based on your DNS records.

Usage

The best way to use DNSFwd is with a CNAME. Add a DNS record like foo.bar.com CNAME baz.com.dnsfwd.com and all requests from foo.bar.com will be 301 redirected to baz.com.

FAQ

How can I trust you with my web traffic?

Well, for one thing, I'm a real person, this domain's WHOIS is my real information, and I don't want to piss anybody off. But that's pretty much it.

Honestly, you just shouldn't use DNSFwd when security is your top priority. It can't support HTTPS anyways.
