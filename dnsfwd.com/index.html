DNSFwd

A simple service that does all kinds of HTTP redirection goodness just based on your DNS records.

Usage

The best way to use DNSFwd is with a CNAME. Add a DNS record like foo.bar.com CNAME baz.com.dnsfwd.com and all requests from foo.bar.com will be 301 redirected to baz.com.
